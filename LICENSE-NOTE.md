author** (the curation, write-ups, and skill adaptations).

They do **not** relicense the underlying methods and third-party work that this
collection documents. Those remain the property of their respective creators and
are governed by their own terms. Each framework entry credits its originators in
an Attribution section — for example, the STORM method belongs to the Stanford

When in doubt, attribute the original source as named in each entry.
